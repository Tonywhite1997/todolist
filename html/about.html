About ToDoList

This is made to track activity progress. It is also made as a project by an aspiring software developer. You can create multiple activities and track your progress with this app. You can also mark an activity complete after it is completed. If you wish to delete completed activites or uncompleted activities, you have a delete option for every activity.

Please note that this app is a work in progress and therfore not stable. Rapid changes may occur anytime. Thank You.
